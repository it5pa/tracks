Tracks as Envelopes was an on-site recording & broadcasting session hosted by oH radio. The session was part of the Dear Friend exhibition at the EKA Gallery. 44 letters were read from the Dear Friend archive by the visitors of the exhibition.

 Dear Friend is a letter format monthly publication initiated by Sandra Nuut and Ott Kagovere.
This audio archive was designed and developed by Paula Buškevica with support from Alejandro Bellón Ample.
oH radio lives within the Graphic Design MA program in Estonian Academy of Arts (EKA).
oH is currently run by Greta Thorkelsdottir & Michael Fowler. It by was initiated in 2021 by Paula Buškevica.
Thanks to all voices of the letters.
